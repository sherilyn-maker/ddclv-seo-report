Root Cause #1 — Average Position 33.7 (Page 3–4)

The #1 driver of the 0.07% CTR is that most pages rank on page 3, 4, or 5 of Google. CTR drops to near-zero beyond page 1: pos 11–20 earns ~0.3–0.5%, pos 21–30 earns ~0.1–0.2%, pos 31+ earns essentially nothing. With 846,618 impressions and only 560 clicks, an estimated 840,000+ impressions are being completely wasted because the site appears but nobody clicks. The solution is getting pages to page 1, not just fixing titles.

💡 The 5 Levers to Fix CTR

Lever 1 — Rank Higher (Position)

Nothing fixes CTR faster than moving from page 3 to page 1. Your average position is 33.7 — the goal is 10 or below for core queries. This requires: content rewrites, FAQ schema, internal linking, and backlinks. See Quick Wins tab for specifics. Expected impact: 5–20× CTR improvement.

Lever 2 — Star Ratings (AggregateRating Schema)

Adding AggregateRating JSON-LD schema to your homepage and service pages causes Google to show yellow stars next to your listing in the SERP. Studies show star ratings increase CTR by 20–35% independent of position. You need real Google reviews to implement this. Target: 4.5+ stars, 50+ reviews visible.

Lever 3 — Offer in Title/Meta ("$49")

For price-sensitive queries like "$49 drain cleaning near me" and "drain cleaning coupons," the number "$49" must appear in your title or meta description. When searchers see "$49" in a listing they are 3–4× more likely to click than a generic description. This is a 5-minute meta description fix.

Lever 4 — FAQPage Schema (Rich Snippets)

FAQPage schema causes your SERP listing to expand with 2–3 dropdown questions directly in Google, taking up twice as much visual space. This alone increases CTR by 15–30% for pages that earn rich results. Add to: homepage, drain cleaning page, sewer camera, hydro jetting, and trenchless repair pages.

Lever 5 — Rewrite Titles to Match Query Intent

Your homepage likely shows "Discount Drain Clean LV | Drain Cleaning" as the title. For someone searching "drain cleaning Las Vegas," the ideal title is: "Drain Cleaning Las Vegas, NV | $49 Special | Same-Day | Licensed." Every word must match what the user typed and what they want to see. Rewrite all 8 core service page titles.
